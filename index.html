Coaching & Consultation

Three tests. Seven services. One cohesive strategy.

Built around the whole applicant — not just the test score.

Our coaching treats IELTS, DET and SAT as craft, not cram. Each student is paired with a senior coach for the full preparation period — diagnostic mocks every fortnight, a custom weakness-map that updates after every session.

Our consultants then handle every admissions touchpoint — from shortlist to scholarship essay to visa mock — so your narrative stays consistent from first coaching class to final passport stamp.

The Stellar Process

Eight deliberate steps, zero guesswork.

From the first discovery call to your first week on campus, every milestone is documented and reviewed. No surprises, no missed deadlines.

01

Discovery Call

A 45-minute video consultation to understand your background, target countries, budget and post-study ambitions. Free, no obligation.

02

Profile Evaluation

A written audit of your transcripts, test scores, work experience and extracurriculars — benchmarked against the last three admission cycles.

03

University Shortlist

Tiered shortlist of 8–12 universities with cohort outcome data and a 20-page written rationale delivered within seven working days.

04

Test Preparation

Begin IELTS, DET or SAT coaching with your dedicated mentor. Fortnightly mocks, weakness-mapping and guaranteed score improvement.

05

Application Craft

Four collaborative essay drafts, LOR briefs, resume rewriting and university-specific supplemental essays reviewed by a senior editor.

06

Submission & Interview

We file every application on your behalf, track acknowledgements and prepare you for programme-specific interviews with two rounds of mocks.

07

Visa Documentation

Complete visa filing with financial document review, sponsor affidavits and consulate-style mock interviews calibrated to your destination country.

08

Pre-Departure Briefing

A final two-hour briefing covering accommodation, banking, SIM, healthcare registration and an introduction to your city's student community.
